The Basic Stats

Little numbers -
big difference.

  • 4,900 loaves of bread are given to hungry children and their families each week
  • 300 Homes Have been provided to families by CCF and World Housing in the last year
  • 2,300 Students Are currently enrolled in CCF's Education Program.
  • 10,823,364 US Dollars were donated to CCF in 2014
  • Hundreds of happy children supported throughout the disease and poverty of Cambodia

CCF has been more than successful throughout the years. It has educated thousands, and fed and nurtured millions more. CCF focuses on the little people, but obviously their families matter as well. Over six hundred cases of cholera, AIDS, and typhoid fever have been succesfully healed wih the intense care medical center, which accompanies CCF on it's journey for happiness.
